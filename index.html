What is Magma Link?

Magma Link is a free hyper-casual browser game where you chain lava nodes together before they cool into solid crust. Tap to cash out your chain at just the right moment!

How to Play

Tap / Space / Enter to start a chain. The chain auto-links to nearby hot nodes on every beat. Tap again to snap (cash out) before the chain hits a cooled crust node and breaks. Walls block line-of-sight — plan your route!

Scoring

Each link in your chain scores points. Longer chains earn a multiplier bonus. An eruption burst fires on cash-out — the bigger the chain, the bigger the burst!

FAQ

Does it save my score? Yes — your best score is saved locally in your browser.

Does it need internet? No — runs offline once loaded.

Mobile friendly? Yes — optimized for touch on phones and tablets.
